The Mission

From the Desk of the Chief of Staff

Science-Informed

We need rigorous evidence, not just ideology. Every proposal must be backed by data that supports the claim.

Workable

Can the supply chain handle it? If we recommend it, can American farmers actually grow it efficiently?

Equitable

Who bears the cost? We must avoid policies that price out low-income families or bankrupt small farms.
